If you've noticed that daily tasks like walking up a single flight of stairs or keeping up with your grandkids are feeling more exhausting than they used to, you are not alone. Millions of people struggle with persistent chest congestion and stubborn phlegm.

Support Healthy Lung Function

Managing respiratory fatigue requires a consistent routine to support your body's natural clearing processes:

  • Stay Hydrated: Drinking plenty of water helps thin the mucus in your lungs, making it easier for your body to clear particles naturally.
  • Gentle Daily Movement: Activities like light walking improve blood flow and encourage deeper breathing.
  • Check Indoor Air Quality: Dust and toxins can force your immune system to overreact, resulting in more trapped phlegm.
